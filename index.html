Who we are
A bookshop built on community, now easier to use than ever.

Silver Halide has been a local favourite for years. Regulars know us for our hand-picked stock, our staff recommendations, and the kind of unhurried browsing that bigger retailers simply cannot offer.

Now you can check what's on the shelf before you leave the house, place an order from anywhere, and still come in to collect it yourself. The shop has not changed. It is just open to more of you.

How each part works

Simple from start to finish.

Finding a book

Search the way you already do at a library.

Every book in the shop has a record in our catalogue. Search by title, author, or ISBN and you will see whether it is in stock. Titles without a standard ISBN are still listed, so nothing slips through the gaps. Stock levels update automatically as books come in, so what you see is always accurate.

Getting in touch

One email. A real reply.

All enquiries come to a single inbox, so nothing gets missed. Whether you are chasing an order, asking for a recommendation, or requesting a title we do not currently stock, you will always reach the right person. We aim to respond the same day.

Ordering online

Local at heart, open to everyone.

The site is built around the shop, not the other way around. Collection is always front and centre because we want you to come in when you can. Delivery is there for when you cannot. Either way, the experience should feel like Silver Halide.

Checking stock

No need to phone ahead.

Stock levels on the site reflect what is actually on the shelf. Search for a title and you will see whether it is available right now. You can search by title, author, or ISBN, just as you would on any library catalogue. No account needed to browse.

Paying and collecting

Order in minutes. Collect the same day.

Place your order online, pay securely, and choose delivery or collection at the shop. Collection orders placed before noon are usually ready the same afternoon. Delivery orders are dispatched the next working day.

Placing an order

Five steps from search to shelf.

Step01

Search the catalogue

Enter a title, author, or ISBN. You will see whether the book is in stock and how many copies are available.

Step02

Choose your book

Select the edition you want and add it to your order. You can keep browsing and add more before you check out.

Step03

Pick delivery or collection

Choose to have your order posted to you, or reserve it for collection at the shop at a time that suits you.

Step04

Pay securely online

We accept Mastercard, Visa, and PayPal. Your payment is processed securely and you will receive a confirmation by email.

Step05

Collect or wait for the post

Collection orders are usually ready the same afternoon. Delivery orders go out the next working day. We will keep you updated either way.
